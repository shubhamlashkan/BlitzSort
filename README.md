# BlitzSort
Drag words into the right category before time runs out. Build your longest streak. One daily puzzle for everyone.

The word-sorting speed game that's easy to learn and impossible to put down.

Blitz Sort is a fast-paced word-sorting game where you drag words into the correct category bucket before the clock hits zero. Every correct sort builds your streak — one wrong move and it resets. Simple to pick up, surprisingly hard to master.
Play Classic mode for unlimited rounds across categories like Animals, Countries, Instruments, and Planets, or take on the Daily Challenge — one puzzle, same for everyone, resets at midnight. Share your emoji result card on X and challenge your friends to beat your streak.
